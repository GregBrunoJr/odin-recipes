Tomato Soup

MMM, MMM, MMM

About this recipe

This is a bowl of tomatoes.

We turned into soup.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar and Salt

Steps

  1. Boil tomatoes, onion, garlic, and broth
  2. Run mixture through a food mill into a large bowl
  3. Make a roux
  4. Add the tomato mixture and season
